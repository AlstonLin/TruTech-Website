Calculall

As youth, we believe that our phones with much higher power and ram should soon take over the antiquated technology of Graphing Calculators. With these ideals, we present Calculall, an Android Applications that brings all of the features of a Graphing Calculator onto your phone. Our dedicated team spent months developing this application to be the most beautiful, effective and efficient tool to use to do complex and simple math on a daily basis.

App Features

Custom Themes
Fractions
Textbook Display

Calculation History
Root Finding
Factoring and Expanding

Differentiation and computing Indefinite Integrals
Vectors
